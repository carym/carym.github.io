Gooseberry Patch Apple Pie with No-Roll Pie Crust 1-1/2 c. all-purpose flour 1t. salt 1/2 c. plus 2 t. sugar, divided 1/2c. oil 2T. milk 4 to 5 cups Jonathan apples, peeled, cored and sliced 1 t. cinnamon In a 9" pie plate, combine flour, salt and 2 teaspoons sugar. Add oil and milk; stir until well blended. Use a spoon to shape dough to fit the pan. Flute edges and set aside. Place apples in a large bowl; set aside. Mix remaining sugar and cinnamon in a cup; sprinkle over apples and toss to coat Spoon apples into crust. Sprinkle Crumb Crust Topping over top. Bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes. Turn oven down to 350 degrees; bake for 30 more minutes. Makes 6 to 8 servings. ----- Crumb Crust Topping: 3/4 c. all-purpose flour 1/2 c. sugar 1/3 c. butter, softened 1/8 t. nutmeg Combine all ingredients; mix with a fork until crumbly.
