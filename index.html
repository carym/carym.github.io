The Honeybadger Bot
Technologies used:
  • turntable.fm API
  • node.js
  • Groovy/Grails

The Honeybadger Bot (HB) was a bot that lived in the "Honeybadger FM" room on the collaborative music website turntable.fm. HB was a repository of the room's favourite jokes, helped enforce good DJing behaviour, and could also interact with the humans and spin music. HB could play music to reflect each user's tastes, or play DJing games.
